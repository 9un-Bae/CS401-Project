Local Resources

Aloha Harvest

Aloha Harvest is a Hawaiʻi-based nonprofit that rescues quality excess food and delivers it free of charge to organizations feeding those in need.

The Pantry

The Pantry is a food distribution service by Feeding Hawaiʻi Together, offering a grocery-style experience where individuals and families can select free, nutritious food with dignity and care.

Hawai'i Food Bank

Hawai‘i Foodbank provides food assistance to families, keiki, and kūpuna across the islands, working with a network of partners to ensure no one in our community goes hungry.
